Bluetooth?

Whats Bluetooth you may ask? well Bluetooth is a form of technology which connects wireless devices and hardware to computers or phones.
These devices include keyboards, headsets, printers, ear buds, fitness bands, stereos and mouses, it acts as a type of portable wi-fi
requiring no connection to the internet, all that is required to access this is another device which souports bluetooth connectivity.
It works in a similar fasion to WI-FI in the sense that they both utilize radio waves, bluetooth uses weaker waves that can only connect via a short distance but is portable and user friendly.
