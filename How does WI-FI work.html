How Does WI-FI Work?

WI-FI works in a similar fashion to mobile phones in which they both utilise radio waves. These waves are used to transmit and
transport imformation across a specific network. Routers acts as type of decrypter that can translate these
signals when transmitted into a network. The router then places this inforation on the
connected device such as a computer or gaming console.
