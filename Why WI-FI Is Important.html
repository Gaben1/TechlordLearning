Why WI-FI is Important

WI-FI and Bluetooth are essential towards advancing in technology, it is the biggest technological invention since the computer.
In this modern era hundreds of jobs requiring and revolving around computing skills and the internet, nearly every
household or business houses a WI-FI router and is connected to the internet. Wireless connectivity isn't the future its now.
